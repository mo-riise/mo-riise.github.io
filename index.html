The Problem

Institutional research existed. Access didn't.

India has over 100 million demat accounts. But high-quality equity research — detailed, analyst-backed, conviction-rated — was locked behind 30-page PDFs, institutional language, and English-only delivery. The gap was never the research. It was accessibility.

The MO Research Suite closes that gap for the first time inside a regulated brokerage context — turning static reports into conversational, real-time intelligence available to every retail investor.

Product 1

Research Home

A dedicated, always-on research surface embedded inside the RIISE trading app. Powered by an automated Lambda-based LLM pipeline that processes every analyst report without human intervention.

  • Live equity and F&O calls curated by MO researchers — updated in real time
  • AI-generated daily market and commodity analysis
  • Every analyst report summarised in 500 words with audio playback
  • News feed with real-time sentiment signals
  • Covers equity, derivatives, and commodities

Product 2

MO Research Assistant

A multi-agent AI assistant invoked from the centre of the app's navigation. Not a chatbot — a reasoning engine with access to live data, 350+ stock reports, 200+ screeners, and technical analysis tools.

  • Live buy/sell recommendations backed by MO analyst research
  • 200+ techno-fundamental smart screeners
  • Technical analysis — RSI, MACD, support and resistance
  • Natural language queries in English, Hindi, or Hinglish
  • Every response cited and traceable to source
  • Median response time: 10 seconds

Compliance & Architecture

Built for a regulated market

Compliance is not a constraint retrofitted at the end — it is a design input from day one.

  • SEBI-compliant framework — responses grounded in published analyst research, not independent AI inference
  • DPDPA compliant by architecture — no client-specific data processed at the AI layer
  • On-premise deployment of client-facing models — no user data transmitted externally
  • Dual-model evaluation — an independent large-scale model evaluates every generation before delivery
  • Live guardrail layer validates numerical accuracy and analyst position alignment on every response
  • Citations provided on every response — fully traceable to source analyst report
  • Full observability stack with live monitoring dashboard across all agent decisions
